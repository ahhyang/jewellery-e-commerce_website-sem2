Return & Exchange Policy

All purchases may qualify for a return or exchange within fourteen 14-days from date of receiving the item(s). Customers may exchange an item for a different colour or size and of equal value only. The item(s) must be in resale-able condition with original packaging. For refund processing an invoice need to be attached upon return. Please submit your request at Product Return and it will be reviewed by GST e-Commerce team. We will arrange to pick up and re-send the item at NO Charge if it is manufacturing fault. For example, or other reasons of return (the shipping charges will be borne by customer). We reserve the right to replace with a new item of the same original order. Shipping charges are not refundable; all points accumulated will be deducted upon refund of the item(s). IF returning an item that qualified you for a free-gift, please include the free-gift with your return. If you choose to keep the free-gift, you will be charged for this value. Please note that your rights to return Products Does NOT apply to product which falls into the following categories, unless they are faulty or not as described;

• Skin Care, Cosmetics and Fragrances

• Undergarments

• Swimwear

• Made to measure or personalised goods

• Footwear and Leather products

• Stationary


An exception can be made for items deemed faulty and cannot be fixed or replaced by the store as per Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU. What is your exchange policy?


• We are unable to accept exchanges online, however you can exchange your goods at any MJ Department Stores physical store within the location and district of purchase. (being prove of payment, or receipt print-out)

• Any purchases made on or after the second week shall be eligible for exchange either until the 15th or 30 days from the date of purchase, whichever is later. You can make the exchange for another product of your choice providing it is priced the same or higher than your original purchase.

• Please ensure that the goods are in resalable condition and that you bring your invoice as proof of purchase. All standard conditions apply.

RETURNS

Please retain your online payment receipt. GST is always happy to exchange or refund all item(s) using the original method of payment as long as the item(s) are returned in a re-sellable condition (with original packaging, tags and/or labels) within 30 days from the date of purchase. If you do not have your original online payment receipt, we will allow customers to make a like for like exchange for the same item, or for the current value of the item, whichever is lower at the time of return. For example, you can change the size or the colour of an item. DEFECTIVE PRODUCTS

If you are unhappy with any of your purchases and feel it may be defective due to a manufacturing fault, please do get in contact with us through our contact from (select topic: SUPPORT). Please do provide as much information as possible concerning the product and the order. If you are a consumer and return a defective Product to us (which must be by post) we will examine the returned Product and will notify you of your refund via e-mail within a reasonable period of time. We will usually process the refund due to you as soon as possible and, in any case within 30 days of the day we confirmed to you via email that you were entitled to a refund for the defective Product. Products returned by you because of a defect will be refunded in full, including a refund of the delivery charges for sending the item to you and the cost incurred by you in returning the item to us.
