About Us

Description of the organization

The Organization's name is GST. The organization is a jewelry store that sells high quality necklaces,bangles and rings.GST imports gold and gems from responsibly sourced as we think everyone should be paid by how much they contribute. GST was founded in 2022 as there was a gap in the gem jewelry market in Malaysia.GST currently holds 80% market share of the Malaysian market enabling GST to keep prices low due to the economy of scale.Economies of scale allows us to produce products at high quantities allowing us to purchase large amounts of resources directly from mines keeping prices cheap.Gst plans to continue expanding to Singapore first,then Thailand and then to Indonesia. There are a total of 9 products on the online website.The website uses a simple black and white theme emphasizing simplicity.
